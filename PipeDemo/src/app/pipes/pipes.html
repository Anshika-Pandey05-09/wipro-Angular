Pure pipes: These pipes only depend on the input values and do not change their output unless the input values change. They are more efficient as Angular can cache their output.

Impure pipes: These pipes can change their output even if the input values are the same. They are less efficient as Angular cannot cache their output and will re-evaluate them on every change detection cycle.
